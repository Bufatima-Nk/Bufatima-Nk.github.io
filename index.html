Hi, I'm Bufatima

Data Science Professional

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a Data Science professional based in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan, with a Master’s degree in Data Science from International Alatoo University. My passion for uncovering insights from data began during my Bachelor’s in Applied Mathematics and Informatics and has grown through hands-on projects and advanced coursework.

My expertise lies in machine learning, time-series forecasting, and data analysis, with a focus on Python (PyTorch, NumPy, Pandas, scikit-learn), and SQL. My Master’s thesis, "Earthquake Forecasting and Major Event Detection Using Multi-Task Deep Learning in Central Asia," showcases my ability to tackle complex problems with innovative solutions.

I’ve completed diverse projects, from predicting air quality in Bishkek using ARMA models to forecasting gold price volatility with GARCH models. These are available on my GitHub, demonstrating my skills in feature engineering, deep learning, and data visualization.

With over two years of experience as a Math, Statistics and Machine Learning Lecturer at AIU IT and Business College, I’ve honed my ability to communicate complex concepts clearly, making me adept at collaborating with cross-functional teams. My certifications from Stanford, DataCamp, and Kaggle further validate my commitment to continuous learning.

I’m eager to join a Data Science team where I can apply my skills to deliver actionable insights, contribute to impactful projects, and grow alongside industry experts. Explore my resume and portfolio to learn more about my work.
