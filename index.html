Hi, I'm Bufatima

Data Science Enthusiast

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm a 25-year-old data science enthusiast based in Kyrgyzstan. My journey into the captivating realm of data science commenced during my university years. Armed with a Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics and Informatics from International Alatoo University, I embarked on this adventure out of sheer curiosity.

It all began when I enrolled in an "Introduction to Data Science" course. This was the spark that ignited my interest and set me on a path of discovery. To this day, that curiosity fuels my passion for data.

I believe in lifelong learning. I've earned certifications from platforms like Coursera and Kaggle, solidifying my dedication to staying updated in this ever-evolving field. My pursuit of knowledge knows no bounds.

In my free time, I love working on data science projects. You can find many of these projects on my GitHub profile. From predicting air quality to crafting models for asset volatility, I apply my skills to real-world challenges.

Currently, I wear two hats – one as a math lecturer at IT&Business College and the other as a dedicated learner. Teaching keeps me motivated, and my thirst for knowledge keeps me exploring new frontiers in data science. It's a delightful balancing act.

When I'm not immersed in data, I enjoy participating in friendly data science competitions. These competitions are a fun way for me to refine my skills and stay sharp.

Now, my focus is on the next chapter of my journey – finding a Data Science internship. My eagerness to apply my skills in a real-world setting, learn from experienced professionals, and contribute to impactful projects knows no bounds.
